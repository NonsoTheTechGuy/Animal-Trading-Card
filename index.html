Max Boerboel

The Boerboel is a large dog, with a strong bone structure and well-developed muscles. It has a large and short muzzle. it has a coat with short sleek dense hair coverage. The recognised colours are brindle, fawn, and brown; it may or may not have a black mask. There is one minor divergence between breed standards; the Kennel Union of South Africa does not accept black, but SABBS does.
